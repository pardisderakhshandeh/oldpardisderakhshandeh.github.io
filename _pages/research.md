## Language Documentation & Mentorship
As a native speaker of Behbahani, I am invested in the documentation of the variety. I recently directed a collaborative initiative—training and leading ten undergraduate researchers in primary data processing—which culminated in the "Linguists, Meet Behbahani" session at the LSA Annual Meeting.
